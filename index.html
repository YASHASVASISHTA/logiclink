Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does it take to get my website?

For all our packages, you'll receive the initial draft within 48 hours after providing all necessary information. The complete website is typically delivered within 5-7 business days, depending on the complexity and your feedback.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes! We provide all our clients with a simple content management guide. For more technical updates, we offer maintenance packages and can handle the updates for you at a discounted rate.

What if I don't like the initial design?

All our packages include revisions. For the Starter Package, you get 3 comprehensive revisions. We'll work with you until you're completely satisfied with the design.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

We include free hosting on Vercel with all our packages. For domain registration, we can guide you through the process or handle it for you (domain registration fees apply).

What happens after my subscription period ends?

We'll contact you before your subscription ends to discuss renewal options. If you choose not to renew, you can either take over hosting yourself or we can provide the source files for a small fee.
